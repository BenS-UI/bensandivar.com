About Me

Creative thinker. Visionary and futurist. Holistic learner.

Raised between America and Europe, I’m a polyglot with a broad cultural perspective. As an artist and a professional, I've spent the past 13+ years shaping ideas that blend these perspectives and cultures into experiences across mediums. From branding systems to interactive tools, my work offers a fresh look with a strong sense of beauty.

Based in Galicia, Spain, I’ve had the privilege of working professionally with clients such as Inditex and Norvento Enerxía, as well as professionals from Repsol, Siemens Gamesa, Abanca, La Voz de Galicia, Estrella Galicia and other professionals and students from across multiple fields including the medical, pharmaceutical, legal, artistic and automotive industries.

Beyond my professional work as a language coach and teacher, I’m driven by curiosity, exploring linguistics, learning software, psychological and neurological insights, and the intersection of classical culture, art, sience and technology to discover new points of synthesis and smarter ways to work, design and build new solutions.

I am also a proliphic artist, writer, musician, music producer, poet and lyricist. All the artistic skills I have acquired over the years have shaped my vision and the way I approach problem-solving and how I face the future. My cultural awareness, multidisciplinary historical, philosophical and spiritual insights, along with my interest in current trends and upcomming technologies help me have an edge and a reliable understanding of the times.
